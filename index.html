Hack-A-Thon: Boommates

My team and I won "Judges' Favorite" for our app, Boommates. We designed an app to help roomates keep track of their chore responsibilities and anonymizes the inevitable reminders from one to another to do their chore. The app uses a system of monetary incentives and integrates with Google Home for ease of use. We are currenty working on perfecting our app and getting it to the app store.

Candy Crush

I implemented a Candy Crush game in C and C++ using GTK+ on a team. We developed a multi-threaded Candy Crush solver that finds the next move resulting in the highest score. For each move, the game queries the solver over a network to find the best possible next move.
